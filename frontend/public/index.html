QUICK ORDER

Using this service you can quickly compile a list of items from our extensive catalog of products.

  1. Simply enter the Taipan item number in the Item # box.
  2. Enter the desired quantity to quickly see a total amount.
  3. Once complete you can click PLACE ORDER to move your list into an order or click on PRINT LIST to save a copy as a Quick Quote reference page.
